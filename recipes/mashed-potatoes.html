Mashed Potatoes

Description

Turn back. You cannot make this. Well, you can't, but I can. Trust me bud, this recipe is reserved only for culinary veterans. Nobody in the world has ever even made this dish before, except for I. If you must, then it is I who will make this recipe in your place. Allow me to guide your hand through making this delightful treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled, or to taste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Heat the milk
  3. Mash the potatoes
