Cranberry Sauce

Description

Man this sauce looks pretty yummy... If only you could cook it for me... Oh? You can? Alright! Let's get cooking!!!!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Boil water in saucepan, add sugar and cook until sugar is dissolved
  3. Stir cranberries into saucepan and bring to boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until cranberries have popped and sauce is chunky
  4. Pour sauce into a serving dish or jars and cover until ready to use
